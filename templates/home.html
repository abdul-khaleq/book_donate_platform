About us
Providentia Books

An online platform to donate &amr; receive books, is designed to disseminate books expected to be donated by book donors and could be used by the book requestors who need them. This online platform includes textbooks, reference books, recreational books, books on career guidance, competitive exam-related books, and the like. This online platform can benefit many people, including impoverished students, teachers, and institutions. This effort by Abdul Khaleq will make the best use of donated books and will help those in need get their books for free if any donated books meet their needs
